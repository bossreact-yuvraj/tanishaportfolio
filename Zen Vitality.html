Research Behind

To create this project did research on symbols and icons related to growth, energy, health, meantal health, and fitness. Also for this thought to use lotus as abstractly lotus symbolises positivity, growth and mental health.

About The Logo & Package

The logo features a minimilistic design, incorporating a lotus flower symbolize growth & purity, along with abstract respresentation of energy. With some plus icons around which symbolise health. Brand stationary for this brand is designed simple yet impactful and linked to the brand.
