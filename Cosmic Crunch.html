Research Behind

This is a brand which has connection with cosmic elements so for this did research for some cpsmic icons and other cosmic related elements which connects with the brands goals and audience. Also for this thought to make little funky logo as it is a youthful brand.

About The Logo & Package

Logo consist of letter C in shape of eaten cookie and a celestial icon. Over it added stars in shape of choco chips to add cookie look like to it. Also added a astrology sign over it to give a cosmic touch.This is a package design for Cosmic Crunch Company. Package is made by keeping in mind all aspects of the particular theme of the company. The background is colour of sky, Cookies are highlighted and the flavors too are highlighted on the package at the front.
