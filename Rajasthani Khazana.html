Rajasthani Khazana

Rajasthani Khazana is a company which sells Rajasthani cuisine. This is totally a self brainstromed project. In this project tried to showcase the elements of Rajasthan through it's packing. For this brand I designed Logo and Package.


Logo Design, Brand Design & Package Design

Research Behind

For this project did research on the culture of Rajasthan, about the art style, food, animals and other important elements which resembles RAJASTHAN. After research brainstromed with some ideas for Logo and package. While creating this project also faced a problem related to how to connect this brand with people and for that here I used hindi font for word Khazana which relates more with the culture and traditions.

About The Logo & Package

Logo is made on the theme of traditional artworks i.e. the lines used in this logo. Lotus here signifies whole culture of Rajasthan.
