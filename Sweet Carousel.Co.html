Sweet Carousel.Co

SWEET CAROUSEL.CO is a cupcake company but not just a normal cupcake company, it is a unique brand who's each flavor represents with famous carnivals of the world. It's Flavor Brazilian Bliss represents carnival of Brazil (Carnaval do Rio de Janeiro), Spanish Fiesta represents carnival of Spain (the Santa Cruz Carnival) and Venice Fruit Medley represents carnival of Italy (Carnevale di Venezia). These Packages are designed by keeping in mind the theme of all the carnivals.Also this a self designed brand.
